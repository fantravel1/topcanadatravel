üó∫Ô∏è Regional Differences

Canada is massive‚Äîtiming varies by region:

  • British Columbia Coast: Mildest winters, rainiest fall/winter, driest summer
  • Canadian Rockies: Snow possible any month at altitude; summer is short (Jul-Aug)
  • Prairies: Extreme temperature swings; very cold winters, hot summers
  • Ontario/Quebec: Hot humid summers, cold snowy winters, spectacular fall
  • Atlantic Canada: Cooler summers, foggy coast, beautiful but brief fall
  • Northern Canada: Extreme cold winters, midnight sun summers, aurora winters
