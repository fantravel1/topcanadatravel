Canada Visa & eTA Guide

Entry Requirements for Visiting Canada

Important: Entry requirements depend on your citizenship. Most visitors need either an eTA (Electronic Travel Authorization) or a visitor visa to enter Canada. U.S. citizens are the exception‚Äîthey need neither for short visits.

Apply well before your trip. While eTAs are often approved within minutes, visas can take weeks or months to process.

üîµ eTA (Electronic Travel Authorization)

The eTA is a simple online application for visitors from visa-exempt countries flying to Canada:

  • Apply at: canada.ca/eta (official government site only!)
  • Cost: $7 CAD (credit card)
  • Processing: Most approved within minutes
  • Valid for: 5 years or until passport expires
  • Allows: Multiple entries, up to 6 months per visit
  • Linked to: Your passport electronically (no stamp needed)

üü¢ Visitor Visa (Temporary Resident Visa)

Citizens of non-visa-exempt countries need a visitor visa:

  • Apply at: canada.ca (online) or Canadian embassy/consulate
  • Cost: $100 CAD per person
  • Processing: Varies widely‚Äîcheck current times for your country
  • Documents needed: Passport, photos, proof of funds, travel plans, ties to home country
  • Biometrics: Required for most applicants ($85 CAD additional)
