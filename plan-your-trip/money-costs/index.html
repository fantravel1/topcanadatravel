üíµ Tipping Guide

Tipping is expected in Canada (similar to USA):

  • Restaurants: 15-20% of pre-tax bill (standard is 18%)
  • Bars: $1-2 per drink or 15-20% of tab
  • Taxis/Uber: 15-20%
  • Hotels: $2-5 per bag for bellhop, $5/day for housekeeping
  • Tour guides: $10-20 per person for day tours
  • Hair/spa: 15-20%
  • Coffee shops: Optional $1-2 or round up

Note: Many restaurants now ask for tips on payment terminals even for counter service. This is optional but increasingly common.

üßæ Taxes

Prices displayed in Canada do NOT include tax. Add these to quoted prices:

  • GST (Federal): 5% everywhere in Canada
  • PST (Provincial): Varies by province (0-10%)
  • HST (Combined): Some provinces combine GST+PST

By Province:

  • Alberta: 5% only (no PST‚Äîcheapest!)
  • BC: 12% (5% GST + 7% PST)
  • Ontario: 13% HST
  • Quebec: 14.975% (5% GST + 9.975% QST)
  • Nova Scotia: 15% HST

üí° Money-Saving Tips

  • Shop in Alberta: No provincial sales tax‚Äîbuy gear here!
  • Parks Canada Discovery Pass: $72/year for unlimited park access (pays off in 7 days)
  • Shoulder season: Visit May/June or September/October for lower prices
  • Cook some meals: Groceries are much cheaper than restaurants
  • Free attractions: Many museums have free evening hours
  • Credit cards: Use no-foreign-transaction-fee cards to avoid 2-3% fees
  • ATMs: Use bank ATMs for best exchange rates; avoid airport exchanges
  • Happy hour: Restaurant deals typically 3-6 PM
