About TopCanadaTravel

TopCanadaTravel is Canada's leading independent travel guide, helping visitors from around the world discover the best destinations, experiences, and travel tips for exploring Canada.

Founded in 2015, TopCanadaTravel has grown from a small blog into a comprehensive travel resource covering over 150 destinations across all 10 provinces and 3 territories. Our team of local experts and experienced travelers provides accurate, up-to-date information to help travelers plan unforgettable Canadian adventures.

We reach over 500,000 monthly visitors from more than 180 countries, with our audience primarily consisting of international travelers planning trips to Canada, as well as Canadians exploring their own country.

TopCanadaTravel is committed to promoting sustainable tourism practices and supporting local communities across Canada. We work closely with tourism boards, national parks, Indigenous tourism organizations, and local businesses to showcase authentic Canadian experiences.

Guidelines

Brand & Usage Guidelines

Logo Usage

When using our logo, please maintain clear space around it and do not alter the colors, proportions, or add effects. The logo should always be clearly visible and not be placed on busy backgrounds.

Referring to TopCanadaTravel

Please use "TopCanadaTravel" as one word with capital T, C, and T. Acceptable references include:

  • TopCanadaTravel (preferred)
  • TopCanadaTravel.com
  • "the Canadian travel guide TopCanadaTravel"

Content Attribution

If quoting or referencing our content, please provide proper attribution with a link back to the original article. For reproduction requests beyond fair use, please contact us.

Image Usage

Press photos provided in our media kit may be used for editorial coverage of TopCanadaTravel. Destination photos on our website are subject to individual licensing and may not be reproduced without permission.
