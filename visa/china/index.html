Chinese Citizens Need Visitor Visa

Chinese passport holders are not eligible for the eTA. You must apply for a Temporary Resident Visa (visitor visa). Since 2014, Chinese citizens can receive 10-year multiple entry visas to Canada.

Application Steps (ç”³è¯·æ­¥éª¤)

1

Create IRCC Online Account

Register at canada.ca and create your account to start the application.

2

Complete Application Forms

Fill out IMM 5257 and IMM 5645 carefully in English or French. All information must match your passport.

3

Upload Documents

Scan and upload all required documents. Translate Chinese documents to English (certified translation recommended).

4

Pay Fees

Pay $185 CAD (visa + biometrics) by credit card. Chinese credit cards (UnionPay), Visa, and Mastercard accepted.

5

Provide Biometrics at VAC

Visit a VFS Global centre in China within 30 days to give fingerprints and photo.

6

Wait for Decision (~33 days)

Track your application online. You may be asked for additional documents or interview.

7

Submit Passport

If approved, submit passport to VFS for visa stamping. Choose pickup or courier delivery.
