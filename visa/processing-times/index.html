Understanding Processing Times

What Affects Processing Time?

  • Application volume from your country
  • Completeness of your application
  • Security screening requirements
  • Seasonal fluctuations (summer rush)
  • Staffing levels at visa offices
  • Requests for additional documents

How Times Are Calculated

IRCC processing times show how long it took to process 80% of applications in recent months. This means:

  • 80% processed within stated time
  • 20% may take longer
  • Some applications processed faster
  • Complex cases take longer

When to Apply

Based on current processing times, we recommend applying:

  • eTA countries: 1-2 weeks before travel
  • Visa (fast): 2-3 months ahead
  • Visa (moderate): 3-4 months ahead
  • Visa (extended): 4-6 months ahead

Track Your Application

Once submitted, you can track your application:

  • Log into your IRCC account
  • Check status updates online
  • Use IRCC web form for inquiries
  • Don't contact IRCC before expected time
