Solo Travel Tips

üõ°Ô∏è Safety

Canada is one of the safest countries for solo travel. Standard precautions apply:

  • Share your itinerary with someone back home
  • Stay aware in unfamiliar areas at night
  • Keep valuables secure in hostel lockers
  • Trust your instincts about situations
  • Emergency: dial 911 anywhere in Canada

ü§ù Meeting People

  • Hostels: Stay in dorms for social atmosphere
  • Walking tours: Free tours attract solo travelers
  • Meetup.com: Local interest groups welcome visitors
  • Bar trivia: Teams often welcome solo joiners
  • Group tours: Day trips guarantee company
  • Hostel events: Most organize pub crawls, dinners

üè® Best Accommodation

  • HI Canada: Network of social hostels
  • Samesun: Popular in BC and Alberta
  • Private room hostels: Privacy + social spaces
  • Airbnb shared spaces: Meet local hosts
  • Co-living spaces: Extended stays with community

üì± Staying Connected

  • Get a local SIM (Lucky Mobile, Public Mobile)
  • Free WiFi widely available
  • Download offline maps before remote areas
  • Consider eSIM for quick setup
  • Share location with family via WhatsApp
