Family Travel Tips

üìÖ Best Time to Visit

Summer (Jul-Aug): School break, warm weather, all attractions open.

Winter Holidays: Ski resorts, holiday events, skating rinks.

March Break: Spring ski conditions, maple syrup season.

Tip: Shoulder seasons have shorter lines and lower prices.

üöó Getting Around

  • Rent a car for flexibility with nap schedules
  • Book car seats in advance with rental companies
  • Consider RV rentals for national park trips
  • VIA Rail has family cars with play areas
  • Many cities have family-friendly transit passes

üè® Family Accommodation

  • Look for suites with kitchenettes
  • Many hotels offer kids-stay-free deals
  • Vacation rentals work well for larger families
  • Parks Canada has oTENTiks (glamping)
  • Book family rooms in hostels for budget stays

üí∞ Saving Money

  • CityPASS saves on multiple attractions
  • Many museums have free admission days
  • Pack picnic lunches for park visits
  • Kids under 17 free in national parks
  • Look for family passes at attractions
