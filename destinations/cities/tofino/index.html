ğŸ„ Surfing in Tofino

Canada's best surfing is here on Vancouver Island's west coast:

  • Best beaches: Cox Bay (consistent waves), Long Beach (beginner-friendly), Chesterman Beach (smaller waves)
  • Water temp: Cold year-round! Wetsuits essential (4/3mm summer, 5/4mm winter)
  • Best waves: Fall and winter bring bigger swells; summer is calmer
  • Lessons: Many surf schools offer 2-3 hour lessons with wetsuit/board ($80-120)
  • Rentals: Wetsuits, boards available throughout town

Tofino invented storm watching as a tourism experience:

  • Season: October through March
  • What to expect: 6-metre waves, 100+ km/h winds, dramatic skies
  • Best views: Wickaninnish Inn, Black Rock Oceanfront Resort, Long Beach Lodge
  • Safety: Watch from lodges or marked viewpointsâ€”rogue waves are deadly
  • Activities: Cozy up by the fire, hot chocolate, spa treatments between storms
  • Package deals: Many hotels offer storm watching packages with extras
