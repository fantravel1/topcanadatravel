The Okanagan produces 84% of BC's wine. Here's how to experience it:

  • Signature wines: Pinot Noir, Chardonnay, Riesling, Merlot, and world-famous icewine
  • Wine trails: Downtown Kelowna, Lakeshore, Westside, and Naramata Bench
  • Top wineries: Mission Hill, Quails' Gate, Summerhill, CedarCreek, and 50th Parallel
  • Tours: Guided bike tours, e-bike tours, bus tours ($100-200 CAD)
  • Harvest season: August-October for grape picking and crush festivals
  • Pro tip: Use a designated driver or book a tourâ€”DUI laws are strict!
