About Vancouver

Vancouver is Canada's gateway to the Pacific, a stunning coastal city surrounded by mountains and ocean. Consistently ranked among the world's most livable cities, it offers an unparalleled combination of urban sophistication and outdoor adventure.

From cycling the Stanley Park seawall to skiing at Grouse Mountain to exploring the multicultural neighborhoods of Chinatown and Commercial Drive, Vancouver rewards exploration. The city is also the gateway to Whistler, Vancouver Island, and the stunning Sea-to-Sky corridor.
