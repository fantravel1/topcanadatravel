Quebec City is primarily French-speaking, more so than Montreal. While tourism workers in Old Quebec generally speak English, knowing basic French phrases is more helpful here. Locals greatly appreciate any effort to speak French ‚Äî even simple greetings like "Bonjour" and "Merci" go a long way. Signs, menus, and information outside main tourist areas are often French-only, but you can navigate comfortably with translation apps.

The Quebec Winter Carnival (Carnaval de Qu√©bec) is the world's largest winter carnival, held annually for 17 days from late January to mid-February. Over 400,000 visitors attend each year. Buy an effigy (small Bonhomme figure) for access to events and discounts.
