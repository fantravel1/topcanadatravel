Explore Winnipeg Neighborhoods

Exchange District

Historic heart with heritage buildings, restaurants, Old Market Square concerts, and Winnipeg's best nightlife. Walk cobblestone streets at night.

The Forks

Where Winnipeg began. Markets, museums, riverside paths, and the world's longest skating trail in winter (9 km on the rivers!).

Osborne Village

Trendy, walkable neighborhood with boutiques, cafes, rainbow crosswalks, and diverse dining. Great for young travelers.

St. Boniface

French Quarter across the river. Visit the St. Boniface Cathedral ruins, French restaurants, and Festival du Voyageur grounds.
