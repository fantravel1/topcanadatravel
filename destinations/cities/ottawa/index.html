Discover Ottawa

Ottawa may be Canada's capital, but it's far from a stuffy government town. This bilingual city straddles the Ontario-Quebec border, blending English and French cultures with a vibrant arts scene, outstanding museums, and stunning natural surroundings.

From the Gothic spires of Parliament Hill to the world's largest naturally frozen skating rink, Ottawa offers experiences you won't find anywhere else. The city punches well above its weight with more museums per capita than any Canadian city ‚Äî and most offer free admission on Thursday evenings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Each season offers unique experiences. May brings the Canadian Tulip Festival with 1 million+ blooms. Summer (June-Aug) has warm weather, Canada Day celebrations (July 1), and outdoor festivals. February features Winterlude when the Rideau Canal becomes the world's largest skating rink. Fall offers spectacular foliage in nearby Gatineau Park.
