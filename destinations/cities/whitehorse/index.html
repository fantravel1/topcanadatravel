Whitehorse is the capital of Yukon Territory and the gateway to some of Canada's most spectacular wilderness. This small city of 28,000 sits on the banks of the Yukon River, surrounded by mountains, forests, and a frontier spirit that dates back to the Klondike Gold Rush of 1898.

Come for the Northern Lights (Whitehorse is one of the best viewing spots in the world), stay for the hot springs, hiking, Gold Rush history, and the start of the legendary Alaska Highway. This is where adventure begins.
