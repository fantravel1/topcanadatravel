Discover Victoria

Victoria is British Columbia's elegant capital city, perched on the southern tip of Vancouver Island. Often called "Canada's most British city," Victoria charms visitors with its afternoon tea tradition, horse-drawn carriages, and impeccably maintained heritage architecture.

But Victoria is much more than British nostalgia. The city is a gateway to incredible outdoor experiences ‚Äî from world-class whale watching to the stunning gardens that earn it the nickname "Garden City." With Canada's mildest climate, Victoria blooms year-round and offers outdoor activities in every season.
