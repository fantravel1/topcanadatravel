â›µ The Legend of Bluenose

The Bluenose is Canada's most famous sailing ship:

  • Original Bluenose: Built in Lunenburg 1921, undefeated racing champion for 17 years
  • Canadian icon: Featured on the dime since 1937 and on stamps
  • Bluenose II: Replica built 1963, rebuilt 2012, homeported in Lunenburg
  • Sailing season: June-September, check schedule at novascotia.com
  • Deck tours: Available when in port (free!)
  • Sailing trips: 2-hour sails when available ($50-75 CAD)
