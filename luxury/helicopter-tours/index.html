Sample Journey

Alpine Glacier Landing - Hour by Hour

Here's what to expect on our most popular experience. Every moment is designed to maximize wonder while ensuring your complete comfort and safety.

9:00
AM

VIP Welcome & Safety Briefing

Arrive at our private helipad in Canmore where you're greeted with fresh coffee and pastries. Your pilot provides a comprehensive safety briefing and explains the day's flight path, tailored based on current weather conditions for optimal views.

9:45
AM

Takeoff & Scenic Flight

Lift off and immediately be surrounded by the Three Sisters peaks. Your pilot narrates as you soar over ancient forests, hidden waterfalls, and pristine alpine lakes. The 25-minute flight to the glacier is an experience unto itself.

10:15
AM

Glacier Landing

Touch down on the ancient ice of the Columbia Icefield. Step out onto snow that fell thousands of years ago. Your guide provides glacier boots and leads a short walk to a viewpoint overlooking the vast ice field stretching to the horizon.

10:45
AM

Champagne Toast & Photography

Pop a bottle of Veuve Clicquot as you take in the silence and immensity of your surroundings. Your guide captures professional photos of your group against this otherworldly backdrop. Savor gourmet chocolates and canapes.

11:30
AM

Return Flight & Landing

A different route home reveals new vistas - perhaps circling a hidden turquoise lake or soaring through a dramatic valley. Land back in Canmore where a digital gallery of your photos awaits, along with a commemorative certificate.
