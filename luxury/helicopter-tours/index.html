Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Private helicopter tours include exclusive use of the aircraft, an experienced pilot/guide, safety equipment, champagne or refreshments, and depending on the package: glacier landings, mountain summit picnics with gourmet cuisine, and professional photography. All flights are fully insured and operated by certified commercial operators.
Helicopter tours operate year-round, but the best conditions are typically June through September for summer scenery and December through March for winter landscapes. Early morning flights often provide the clearest visibility and most dramatic lighting. Winter flights offer stunning snow-covered peaks and frozen waterfalls.
Most luxury helicopter tours accommodate 2-6 passengers depending on the aircraft model. For larger groups, multiple helicopters can be arranged to fly in formation. Weight restrictions apply for glacier landings, and our concierge team will ensure appropriate aircraft selection.
Safety is paramount. If weather conditions prevent safe flying, tours can be rescheduled at no additional cost. Our pilots make final weather decisions on the day of flight. We recommend booking with flexibility in your schedule when possible.
