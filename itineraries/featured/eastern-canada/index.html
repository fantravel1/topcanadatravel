Trip Overview

This classic Eastern Canada itinerary takes you through two provinces and five unforgettable cities. Experience the best of Ontario and Quebec ‚Äî from Toronto's multicultural buzz and Niagara Falls' raw power to Ottawa's national treasures, Montreal's European flair, and Quebec City's 400 years of history.
