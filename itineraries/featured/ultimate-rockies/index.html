Ultimate Canadian Rockies

Trip Overview

This is the definitive Canadian Rockies experience - a 10-day journey through some of the most spectacular mountain scenery on Earth. From the turquoise waters of Lake Louise to the dramatic peaks along the Icefields Parkway, this trip showcases the very best of Banff and Jasper National Parks.

You'll hike to hidden teahouses, watch for grizzly bears and elk, soak in natural hot springs, and drive one of the world's most scenic highways. Whether you're a first-time visitor or returning for more, this itinerary balances must-see highlights with lesser-known gems.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

1

Arrive Calgary, Drive to Banff

Calgary Airport ‚Üí Banff (128 km, 1.5 hrs)

Morning/Afternoon

Arrive at Calgary International Airport (YYC) and pick up your rental car. Make sure you have a Parks Canada pass before entering the park - you can buy one at the park gate or online.

Evening

Arrive in Banff and check into your hotel. Explore Banff Avenue for dinner and get oriented with this charming mountain town.

  • Pick up groceries and snacks at the Banff IGA
  • Walk along the Bow River trail at sunset
  • Dinner at Park Distillery or Bear Street Tavern

üí° Tip: Book your Lake Louise and Moraine Lake parking passes NOW if visiting May-October. They sell out months in advance!

2

Banff Exploration

Full day in Banff

Morning

Ride the Banff Gondola to the top of Sulphur Mountain for panoramic views of six mountain ranges. Walk the boardwalk to Sanson's Peak (1km).

Afternoon

Visit Bow Falls and walk to the historic Fairmont Banff Springs Hotel. Drive up Tunnel Mountain for sunset views.

Evening

Soak in the Banff Upper Hot Springs to relax tired muscles before dinner downtown.

  • Banff Gondola: $75 CAD adult
  • Hot Springs: $10 CAD adult
  • Allow 3-4 hours for the gondola experience
3

Johnston Canyon & Vermilion Lakes

Hiking day in Banff area

Morning

Early start for Johnston Canyon - one of Banff's most popular hikes. Walk the catwalk trail to the Lower Falls (1.1km) and Upper Falls (2.7km). For adventurous hikers, continue to the Ink Pots (5.8km total).

Afternoon

Drive to Vermilion Lakes for stunning reflection photos with Mount Rundle. Stop at Two Jack Lake for a picnic.

  • Start Johnston Canyon by 7am to avoid crowds
  • Trail difficulty: Easy to Upper Falls, Moderate to Ink Pots
  • Vermilion Lakes: Best photos at sunrise/sunset
4

Lake Louise

Banff ‚Üí Lake Louise (57 km)

Morning

Arrive at Lake Louise before sunrise for the most magical experience. Watch the lake transform as morning light hits Victoria Glacier.

Afternoon

Hike the Plain of Six Glaciers trail (13.8km return) to the historic teahouse serving homemade treats. Alternatively, take the shorter Fairview Lookout trail (2.1km) for aerial views.

Evening

Check into your Lake Louise accommodation and enjoy dinner with mountain views.

üí° Pro Tip: The teahouse at Plain of Six Glaciers is cash only. They serve fresh-baked pie and tea - don't miss it!

5

Moraine Lake & Lake Agnes

Full day at Lake Louise area

Early Morning

Take the shuttle or drive (with reservation) to Moraine Lake for sunrise at the famous "Twenty Dollar View." Climb to the Rockpile viewpoint for the classic Valley of Ten Peaks shot.

Afternoon

Return to Lake Louise and hike to Lake Agnes Tea House (7km return). The trail passes Mirror Lake and offers stunning alpine scenery.

  • Moraine Lake opens late May/early June (snow-dependent)
  • Lake Agnes Tea House: Cash only, homemade soup & scones
  • Option: Continue to Big Beehive for panoramic views
6

Icefields Parkway

Lake Louise ‚Üí Jasper (232 km)

Full Day

Drive the legendary Icefields Parkway (Highway 93N) - often called the most beautiful road in the world. Take your time with multiple stops.

Must-Stop Points

  • Bow Lake: Stunning turquoise lake just off the highway
  • Peyto Lake: Wolf-shaped lake with incredible blue color (short hike to viewpoint)
  • Columbia Icefield: Walk to the toe of Athabasca Glacier or book the Ice Explorer tour
  • Sunwapta Falls: Powerful waterfall easy to access from parking lot
  • Athabasca Falls: Thundering falls just before Jasper

üí° Note: No cell service for most of this drive. Download offline maps and fill your gas tank before departing Lake Louise.

7

Jasper Arrival

Exploring Jasper town & area

Morning

Sleep in after yesterday's long drive. Explore the charming town of Jasper and pick up supplies.

Afternoon

Visit Pyramid Lake and Pyramid Island (easy 1km walk across the bridge). Great spot for kayaking or canoeing.

Evening

Take a wildlife drive on Highway 16 toward Pocahontas - excellent chances of seeing elk, deer, and bears.

  • Jasper is a Dark Sky Preserve - perfect for stargazing
  • Book tomorrow's Maligne Lake cruise today
8

Maligne Lake & Canyon

Full day excursion (90 km round trip)

Morning

Drive the scenic Maligne Lake Road, stopping at Medicine Lake (disappearing lake!) along the way.

Midday

Take the boat cruise to Spirit Island - the most photographed spot in the Canadian Rockies. The 90-minute cruise is worth every penny.

Afternoon

On the return, stop at Maligne Canyon for an easy walk along the rim. The canyon is up to 50m deep with multiple waterfall viewpoints.

  • Spirit Island cruise: ~$80 CAD adult
  • Maligne Canyon: 5 bridges loop, 3.7km, 1-2 hours
9

Jasper Adventure Day

Choose your adventure

Options

  • Hiking: Valley of the Five Lakes (4.5km loop) or Sulphur Skyline (8km, challenging)
  • Rafting: Whitewater rafting on Athabasca River (Class II-III)
  • Jasper SkyTram: Ride to 2,263m for views and alpine hiking
  • Miette Hot Springs: Hottest natural springs in the Rockies

Evening

Final dinner in Jasper. Try The Raven Bistro or Evil Dave's Grill for locally-sourced cuisine.

10

Return to Calgary

Jasper ‚Üí Calgary (415 km, 4.5 hrs)

Route Options

  • Via Edmonton (Hwy 16): Fastest route, 4.5 hours. Stop at Elk Island National Park for bison
  • Via David Thompson Hwy (Hwy 11): Scenic alternative, 5.5 hours, fewer crowds
  • Back via Icefields Parkway: Longest but most scenic, 6+ hours

Calgary Departure

Allow 2 hours before your flight for car return and check-in at YYC airport.
