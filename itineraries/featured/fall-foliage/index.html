üçÅ Peak Foliage Timing

Fall colors progress from north to south as temperatures drop:

  • Northern Ontario/Quebec: Mid-September (first to turn)
  • Algonquin Park: Late September to early October (peak)
  • Muskoka: Late September to mid-October
  • Ottawa/Gatineau: Early to mid-October
  • Southern Ontario: Mid to late October
  • Quebec City area: Early to mid-October

Best strategy: Plan your trip for late September to mid-October to catch multiple regions at or near peak!
