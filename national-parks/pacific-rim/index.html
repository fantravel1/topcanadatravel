Discover Pacific Rim

Pacific Rim National Park Reserve protects one of the most spectacular coastal environments on Earth. Located on Vancouver Island's wild west coast, the park encompasses ancient temperate rainforest, rugged shorelines, and rich marine ecosystems where the forest literally meets the sea.

Whether you're surfing Long Beach, hiking through 800-year-old cedar groves, watching grey whales migrate, or experiencing the raw power of Pacific storms, this park offers experiences found nowhere else in Canada.
