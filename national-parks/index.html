Frequently Asked Questions

Daily admission costs $10.50 CAD for adults, $9.00 for seniors (65+), and is free for youth 17 and under. The Parks Canada Discovery Pass costs $72.25 CAD for adults and provides unlimited admission to all national parks and historic sites for one year. Family/group passes are available for $145.25 CAD.
Banff National Park is often considered Canada's best, famous for Lake Louise and Moraine Lake. However, the "best" depends on your interests: Jasper for stargazing and fewer crowds, Pacific Rim for surfing and rainforests, Gros Morne for hiking and fjords, or Nahanni for remote wilderness adventures.
Reservations are required for camping in most parks and recommended for popular backcountry trails. Some parks like Banff require parking reservations for Lake Louise and Moraine Lake during peak season (May-October). Book through the Parks Canada reservation system at reservation.pc.gc.ca.
