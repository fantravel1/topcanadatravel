Planning Your Alberta Trip

üå§Ô∏è Best Time to Visit

Summer (Jun-Aug): Hiking season, all roads open, warm days and cool nights.

Fall (Sep-Oct): Golden larches, fewer crowds, crisp weather.

Winter (Nov-Apr): World-class skiing at Lake Louise, Sunshine, Marmot Basin.

Spring (May): Shoulder season, some trails still snowy, wildlife emerging.

üöó Getting Around

Calgary Airport (YYC): Main gateway, 90 min to Banff.

Car Rental: Essential for exploring parks and scenic drives.

Shuttles: Bus services run between Calgary, Banff, and Jasper.

Parks Canada Pass: Required for all national parks.

üìç Must-See Spots

  • Moraine Lake - Iconic turquoise waters
  • Lake Louise - Most photographed lake
  • Peyto Lake - Stunning viewpoint
  • Columbia Icefield - Walk on ancient glaciers
  • Johnston Canyon - Easy scenic hike
  • Maligne Lake - Spirit Island boat tour
