Planning Your BC Trip

üå§Ô∏è Best Time to Visit

Summer (Jun-Aug): Best weather, all activities open. Busy season.

Fall (Sep-Oct): Fewer crowds, salmon runs, wine harvest.

Winter (Dec-Mar): Ski season, storm watching in Tofino.

Spring (Apr-May): Cherry blossoms in Vancouver, quiet season.

üöó Getting Around

Vancouver: Excellent transit (SkyTrain, buses, SeaBus).

Rest of BC: Car essential for exploring beyond cities.

Ferries: BC Ferries connects Vancouver Island and Gulf Islands.

Flights: Floatplanes connect remote coastal communities.

üìç Regional Highlights

  • Sea-to-Sky - Whistler, Squamish, hiking
  • Okanagan - Wine tours, beaches, fruit orchards
  • Kootenays - Hot springs, skiing, mountain culture
  • Sunshine Coast - Quiet coastal communities
