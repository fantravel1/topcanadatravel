About Golf in Kelowna

The Kelowna area is one of Canada's premier golf destinations, with 19 courses set against the spectacular backdrop of Okanagan Lake, vineyards, orchards, and rolling mountains. The region's warm, dry climate provides ideal golfing conditions from April through October, with over 2,000 hours of annual sunshine meaning rain delays are rare.

From championship layouts designed by Canada's top golf architects to affordable public courses perfect for casual rounds, the Okanagan offers incredible variety. Many courses weave through vineyards and orchards, creating a uniquely Okanagan experience you won't find anywhere else. Add in the ability to pair your round with wine touring, and you have the ingredients for a perfect golf vacation.

Green fees are excellent value compared to similar-quality courses elsewhere in North America, particularly during shoulder season (April-May and September-October) when rates drop significantly and the weather is still excellent for golf.

Golf & Wine Packages

Several Kelowna resorts and hotels offer combined golf-and-wine packages that include tee times at top courses, wine touring, and accommodation. These packages represent excellent value and take the planning hassle out of a golf vacation. Check with local tourism operators for current offerings.

Top Courses

Harvest Golf Club

Consistently ranked among BC's top courses, Harvest Golf Club features a stunning layout winding through orchards and vineyards with panoramic views of Okanagan Lake. The par-72 championship course plays 7,109 yards from the tips and offers a fair challenge for all skill levels. The clubhouse restaurant is outstanding -- many non-golfers visit just for the dining and views.

Gallagher's Canyon Golf & Country Club

One of the Okanagan's most dramatic courses, Gallagher's Canyon features holes carved through ponderosa pine forests along the rim of a deep canyon. The signature par-3 5th hole plays across a ravine with a 100-foot drop. The course also includes The Pinnacle, a 9-hole par-32 short course perfect for beginners or a quick round. Owned by the same group as the Okanagan Golf Club.

Okanagan Golf Club - Bear Course

Designed by the legendary Nicklaus Design Group, the Bear Course is a challenging championship layout with dramatic elevation changes and strategic bunkering. Multiple tee boxes make it playable for all levels, but the back tees will test even single-digit handicappers. The views of the valley from the upper holes are breathtaking.

Okanagan Golf Club - Quail Course

The Quail Course is a Les Furber design that is more forgiving than its Bear neighbour while still providing a quality championship experience. The course winds through natural terrain with water features and well-placed hazards. It is a popular choice for mid-handicap golfers looking for an enjoyable but not punishing round.

Two Eagles Golf Course

Located on the Westbank First Nation land overlooking Okanagan Lake, Two Eagles offers one of the most scenic rounds in the region. The Links-style layout with native grasses and open terrain creates a different feel from the tree-lined courses. Excellent value with some of the most affordable green fees among top-tier courses.

Booking Tips

Book tee times 2-4 weeks in advance during peak season (July-August). Shoulder season (April-May, September-October) offers 30-50% savings on green fees with equally great weather. Twilight rates (after 2-3 PM) save 30-40% at most courses. Many courses offer online booking discounts.
